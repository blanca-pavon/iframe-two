ANALYSIS OF RESULTS
The plots show the force exerted by the piston and its pressure equivalent at different heights of the tank. Even though, by looking at the plots, it can be seen that neither the force nor the pressure reached the targets of 22.56 N and 50 mbar respectively.

In spite of this, these experiments were qualified as satisfactory. The reason behind this is that the main objective was fulfilled: a working mechanism that provides a constant force is able to pressurise the tank while using a reduced space. The closely constant pressure can be clearly seen in the plot.
